Getting your export from WhatsApp

  1. 1Open the group in WhatsApp and tap the group name.
  2. 2Choose Export Chat, then Without Media.
  3. 3Save the .txt or .zip and drop it above.

Pick Without Media. WhatsApp caps exports at roughly 40,000 messages without media but only about 10,000 with it, counting back from the most recent — so a with-media export can quietly drop most of your history.
